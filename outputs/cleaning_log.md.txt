# Cleaning Log

## Issues Found

- Missing Region values
- Missing Ship Mode values
- Missing Discount values
- Mixed date formats
- Inconsistent text formatting
- Potential duplicate records

## Cleaning Actions

- Standardized customer names.
- Standardized text fields.
- Filled missing Region with "Unknown".
- Filled missing Ship Mode with "Unknown".
- Replaced missing Discount with 0.
- Calculated shipping delay days.
- Created calculated sales, profit, and profit margin.
- Created order month and order year.

## Business Rules Applied

- Cancelled orders excluded from completed sales.
- Failed payments excluded from completed sales.
- Refunded orders summarized separately.
- Invalid shipping records flagged.

## Assumptions

- Raw dataset remained unchanged.
- Blank discounts treated as 0.

## Records Removed

None.

## Records Flagged

Records with invalid dates or business rule violations were flagged.

## Limitations

Some source records contained mixed date formats that required flagging instead of automatic correction.
